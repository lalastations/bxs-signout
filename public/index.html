BIGXSHOW App Information

App Name: BIGXSHOW

Developer: HyperDX Inc.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to My Page from the main menu.
  3. Go to the Personal Information page.
  4. Select Edit Information and then choose Delete Account.
  5. Provide the reason for account deletion by entering or selecting an option.
  6. Complete the phone number verification process to confirm your identity.
  7. Once verified, your account information will be permanently deleted.

If you encounter any issues during this process, please reach out to our support team below.

Data Deletion & Retention Policy

When you request to delete your account, the following data handling policy will be applied:

  • Deleted Data: All personal information will be deleted immediately upon account deletion.
  • Retained Data: Order history will be retained for 5 years to comply with legal and financial regulations, after which it will be permanently deleted.
