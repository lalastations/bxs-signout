Account Deletion & Customer Support

BIGXSHOW How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account from within the app, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to My Page from the main menu.
  3. Go to the Personal Information page.
  4. Select Edit Information and then choose Delete Account.
  5. Provide the reason for account deletion by entering or selecting an option.
  6. Complete the phone number verification process to confirm your identity.
  7. Once verified, your account information will be permanently deleted.

If you encounter any issues during this process, please reach out to our support team below.
